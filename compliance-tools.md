# Compliance & Regulatory SaaS Tools

A curated list of SaaS tools helping businesses navigate EU regulatory compliance.

## Digital Product Passport (DPP)

The EU's Digital Product Passport regulation (part of the Ecodesign for Sustainable Products Regulation) requires products to carry a digital identity with environmental data.

| Tool | Description | Free Tier |
|------|-------------|-----------|
| [DPP Tool](https://dpp-tool.com) | Generate and validate Digital Product Passports compliant with EU ESPR regulations | Yes |
| [GreenClaims Scanner](https://greenclaims-scanner.com) | Verify environmental claims against EU Green Claims Directive requirements | Yes |
