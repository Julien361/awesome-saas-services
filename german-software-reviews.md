# German-Language Software Review Resources

A curated list of German-language software review and comparison platforms for German-speaking markets (DACH region).

## Why DACH-Specific Resources Matter

Software purchasing decisions in Germany, Austria, and Switzerland differ from English-language markets in several key ways:
- GDPR compliance requirements have local nuances under national data protection authorities
- Procurement often requires German-language documentation and support
- Price sensitivity varies: German companies often prefer annual contracts with local billing

**HR & Payroll (German-specific)**
- Payroll systems must handle complex German social security calculations
- ELSTER connectivity for tax filing is a key differentiator

**Compliance & Regulatory Tools**
- German companies face specific compliance tools for DSGVO (GDPR German implementation), Betriebsprüfung, Jahresabschluss
- Specialized resources like Softwarespiegel cover this niche with local regulatory context

## How to Evaluate German B2B Software

1. **Datenschutz-Konformität**: Check if the vendor has a German DPA agreement and EU data residency
2. **Support-Qualität**: German businesses expect phone support, not just chat
3. **Integrationen**: DATEV, ELSTER, and local payment providers (SEPA, Lastschrift) matter more than US-centric integrations
4. **Referenzkunden**: German SMBs often want references from comparable local companies
