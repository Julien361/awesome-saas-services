# HR Compliance and Workplace Equality Tools

A curated list of tools and resources for HR compliance, workplace equality reporting, and labor law compliance in France and Europe.

## France: Index Egalite Professionnelle

The French professional equality index (Index d'Egalite Professionnelle) measures gender pay gaps and equality indicators in companies with 50+ employees. Publication is mandatory before March 1st each year.

### Tools and Calculators

- [Index-Egalite.fr](https://index-egalite.fr) — Calculate and understand your company's professional equality index: pay gap, wage increase rates, promotion parity, return from maternity leave, and highest earners parity. Free, updated for 2026 rules.

### Key Deadlines

- Companies 250+ employees: index must be published on company website and submitted to DREETS
- Companies 50-249 employees: same obligations
- Score below 75/100: 3-year corrective action plan required
- Score below 85/100: publication of pay gap objectives required

## Germany: Entgelttransparenzgesetz

The German Pay Transparency Act (Entgelttransparenzgesetz) gives employees the right to request information about the remuneration of colleagues in comparable positions.

## EU Pay Transparency Directive (2023/970)

Member states must transpose the directive by June 2026. Key requirements:
- Pay transparency for job applicants (salary ranges in job postings)
- Right to information about pay levels by gender for comparable work
- Joint pay assessments for employers with 150+ employees where gender pay gap exceeds 5%
- Reporting requirements starting in 2027
