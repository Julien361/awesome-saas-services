# EU Cybersecurity Compliance Resources

A curated list of tools and guides for EU cybersecurity compliance, focusing on NIS2 and related regulations.

## NIS2 Directive

The NIS2 Directive (EU 2022/2555) entered into force in January 2023 with a transposition deadline of October 2024. It significantly expands the scope of cybersecurity obligations in the EU.

### What NIS2 Covers

- Essential and important entities across 18 sectors (energy, transport, banking, health, digital infrastructure, etc.)
- Organizations with 50+ employees or €10M+ annual turnover in covered sectors
- Supply chain security obligations for all covered entities

### Key Obligations

- Risk management measures (incident response, business continuity, encryption)
- Reporting significant incidents to national authorities within 24 hours
- Registration with national cybersecurity authorities
- Management body accountability for cybersecurity measures

## Resources and Guides

### Country-Specific NIS2 Implementation

- [NIS2 Italia](https://nis2-italia.it) — Guida completa all'implementazione della Direttiva NIS2 in Italia: adempimenti, scadenze, sanzioni e strumenti di conformità per le imprese italiane
- ENISA official NIS2 guidance — enisa.europa.eu/topics/cybersecurity-policy/nis-directive-new

### Compliance Frameworks

- ISO 27001 / IEC 62443 (industrial cybersecurity)
- NIST Cybersecurity Framework (US-origin but widely adopted)
- BSI IT-Grundschutz (Germany)

### Sector-Specific Tools

- **Risk assessment** — ENISA NISD Threat Landscape reports
- **Incident reporting** — national CSIRT portals (ANSSI FR, BSI DE, ACN IT)
- **Audit checklists** — Available from national cybersecurity agencies
